FAQs

What happens if I am interested in having you tutor me or my student?

I have a free virtual meeting with you and your student (if the person is a minor) and we see if we are a good fit. This session is usually less than an hour long. We go over what to expect with tutoring. This session is free and no commitment is necessary at this meeting.

Where does tutoring take place?

Tutoring is available online or in person in Lis’ tutoring space.

What do you charge for tutoring?

It depends – I charge $100/session for in-person tutoring and $90/session for virtual tutoring, but if your family is struggling financially, I hold a few spots at low or no cost for students- please let me know.

How do I pay you?

Families pay via PayPal when you schedule a tutoring session.

How long are sessions?

Tutoring sessions are 55 minutes.

What grades do you tutor?

Sixth grade and up.

What subjects do you tutor?

Organizational Skills/Study Skills, Physics, AP Earth Sciences, Math (8th grade to college intro),* English, and Hebrew for Bar and Bat Mitzvahs. *Math levels include – Integrated Math 1, Integrated Math 2, Integrated Math 3, Algebra 1, Geometry, Algebra 2
