FAQs

What happens first?

Lis meets virtually with the student and family for a free 30-minute consult. You can talk through goals, logistics, and whether tutoring is a good fit.

Where does tutoring happen?

Students can meet online or in person in Lis’s tutoring space.

What do sessions cost?

Virtual sessions are $90. In-person sessions are $100. Sessions are 55 minutes.

How do I pay?

Families pay when scheduling a tutoring session.

What grades do you tutor?

Sixth grade and up, including high school and college intro courses.

What subjects do you tutor?

Math, physics, AP Earth Science, study skills, organization, Hebrew, and Bat / Bar Mitzvah preparation.

Do you offer financial flexibility?

Yes. A limited number of low- or no-cost spots may be available for families who need support.

Still unsure?

The free consult is the simplest way to ask questions and decide next steps.
