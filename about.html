About Lis

Lis Cohen brings a calm, clear teaching style to students who need help making school feel more manageable.

She has taught and trained students, scientists, engineers, policy makers, and community audiences. Her background includes science education, meteorology, climate and environmental policy, and Hebrew tutoring for Bat / Bar Mitzvah students.

Lis holds master’s degrees in public affairs from Princeton University and meteorology from the University of Utah. She earned her bachelor’s degree at Cornell University, where she studied Atmospheric Science and the Science of Earth Systems.
