FAQs

How do we start?

New families start with a free 30-minute virtual consult. You can ask questions, share what is going on, and see whether tutoring feels like a good fit.

Where does tutoring happen?

Tutoring is available online or in person in Lis’ tutoring space.

How much does tutoring cost?

Virtual tutoring is $90 per session. In-person tutoring is $100 per session.

How long are sessions?

Tutoring sessions are 55 minutes.

What grades do you tutor?

Lis works with students in sixth grade and up, including middle school, high school, and college students.

What subjects do you tutor?

Math, physics, AP Earth Science, study skills, organization, and Hebrew for bar and bat mitzvah preparation.

How do families pay?

Families pay when they schedule a tutoring session.

Start with the free 30-minute consult.
