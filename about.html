About Lis

Experienced, patient tutoring with a science educator’s mindset.

Lis Cohen helps students slow down, understand what matters, and build confidence one step at a time.

Meet Elisabeth “Lis” Cohen.

Lis enjoys tutoring students in the Denver area and online. She has extensive experience teaching, training, and translating complex ideas into clear, manageable steps.

Today, Lis works part-time training scientists, engineers, policy makers, and the public as part of the COMET program at the University Corporation for Atmospheric Research. She also tutors students in Hebrew for bar and bat mitzvah preparation.

Why students work well with Lis

Lis brings warmth, structure, and deep subject knowledge to each session. Her goal is not just to get through the assignment, but to help students understand how to approach the next challenge more independently.

  • Clear explanations without judgment
  • Strong science, math, and teaching background
  • Support for both content and study habits
