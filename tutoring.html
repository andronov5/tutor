Session rhythm

Every session has a purpose.

Sessions are structured enough to stay productive and flexible enough to meet the student where they are that day.

1

Clarify

Identify what feels confusing and what needs attention first.

2

Practice

Work through problems, assignments, or skills with clear guidance.

3

Leave with a plan

End with a simple next step so the student knows how to keep going.
